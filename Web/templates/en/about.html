How it works

  1. 1. Choose category: Click on the appropriate category.
  2. 2. Choose subcategory
  3. 4. Enter words to search for, separated by commas: Automatically searches in both Macedonian and Latin.
  4. 4.1. Choose search method from the dropdown
  5. 4.2. Choose sorting method (optional)

Why this site exists

This site exists because I was annoyed that all Macedonian websites for ads search only by title and don't have good search logic, so this site solves that by allowing search by title and description and having more options for search methods, I hope this site will be useful to someone
